SDPI MASS MAILING CAMPAIGN

Keezhuthoni Ward Committee

We are launching this mass mailing campaign to address the critical issue of the abandoned PMGSY road construction in Keezhuthoni Ward, Ittiva Panchayat. This vital infrastructure project, which was meant to connect several villages including Akkoanam, Pulimoodu, Kundayam, Kizhuthoni, and Aanappara, has been left incomplete, causing immense hardship to the local residents.

The road, officially named MRL08-Manjapara-Kundayam-Pulimood-Akkonam-keezhthoni-Anapara-Mukkada-Palamon Road (Balance work) under Package no: KR0667 in Kollam district, has become nearly impassable due to the contractor abandoning the work midway. This has created dangerous conditions for vehicles and pedestrians alike, especially affecting the children attending the Government LP School in the area.

While two other roads under the same tender were reassigned to a new contractor through re-tendering, our road remains neglected with no progress. With the monsoon season approaching, we fear the situation will deteriorate further, potentially cutting off access for hundreds of families who depend on this route for their daily needs, medical emergencies, and school transportation.

This campaign aims to flood the concerned authorities with emails demanding immediate action to resume and complete this crucial infrastructure project. We cannot afford to wait any longer while our community suffers from this neglect. Your participation in this mass mailing campaign can make a real difference in getting this work restarted.

Join us in demanding accountability and action from the PMGSY authorities. Together, we can ensure that our voices are heard and that this vital road construction is completed without further delay.
